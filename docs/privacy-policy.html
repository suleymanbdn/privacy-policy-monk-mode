Monk Mode

Privacy Policy

Overview

Monk Mode ("the App") is a focus timer and dopamine detox tracker. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Focus session history (duration, date)
  • Streak count and longest streak
  • Dopamine score
  • Total focus minutes and session count

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. If you uninstall the App, all local data is permanently deleted.

Internet Usage

The App uses an internet connection solely when you choose to share your session results using the built-in share feature. No data is sent automatically or in the background.

Third-Party Services

The App currently does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If third-party services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified through an App update.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, data security is managed by your device's operating system. We recommend keeping your device's software up to date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
